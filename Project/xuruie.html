“

    Hello World
    Medium: Installation, Digital Image
    Materials: Electronic Component Dimension: 40*40*26CM
    Date: 05/2021
    In the era of big data, data analysis technology has completely penetrated into every one of our
    lives with the popularization of various smart wearable devices. I think when human beings create and enjoy technology they are also unknowingly kidnapped by it. The effect of “Information Cocoon” caused by data and algorithm is more and more obvious and even gradually become a symbol of power.

    &Starting from the most representative data terminal -- display, I used discarded electronic components and usable display as the main materials to make a miniature scene of the city. In the miniature of the city, the display is the only electronic component that can be used. I distinguish it from other discarded electronic components in this point, and set its geographical location in an absolutely central, tall position, trying to create a dystopian atmosphere. In this way, I hope to show the monitoring and domination of data and algorithms in our daily life, and to draw public attention to this phenomenon because it is definitely not a technical problem.
